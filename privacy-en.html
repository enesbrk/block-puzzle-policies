1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to Block Puzzle Flow (“App”). We respect your privacy. The App does not request personal identifiers (such as name or email) for core functionality.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account/Registration: We do not ask users to create an account or submit personal information.
  • In-App Data: Game progress may be stored locally on your device.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash reports may be available via platform providers (Apple/Google).

3. Third-Party Advertising Services

We may show ads in the future (e.g., Google AdMob, Unity Ads, Meta Audience Network). These providers may process data such as advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID), approximate location (IP-based), device and usage information under their own privacy policies.

Personalized Ads Controls:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising / Tracking to limit personalization.
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Turn off ad personalization.

We do not control third parties’ processing. Refer to their privacy notices for details.

4. Cookies & Tracking

The App itself does not use cookies; ad SDKs may use device identifiers and similar technologies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Personalized ads to children are disabled.

6. Your Rights (GDPR/Local Laws)

While we do not directly process personal data, you may exercise rights (e.g., access, deletion, objection) with third-party ad providers. Contact us and we will reasonably assist.

7. Data Security & Retention

Local game data remains on your device. Ad providers’ retention periods are governed by their own policies.

8. International Transfers

Ad providers may transfer data internationally subject to appropriate safeguards as set out in their policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Updates are effective upon posting on this page.
